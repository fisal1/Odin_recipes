Meen Kuzhambu Recipes

Description

Fish Kulambu is one of the popular South Indian curries which has tamarind and coconut as main ingredients. Fish Kuzhambu can be made using pomfret fish or vanjaram fish etc. Vegetarians can try this curry with brinjals, small onion, garlic, arbi etc.

Ingredients

  • 500g fish (cleaned and cut)
  • 2 tbsp oil
  • 1 onion (sliced)
  • 2 tomatoes (chopped)
  • 1 tbsp ginger-garlic paste
  • 1 tsp red chili powder
  • 1/2 tsp turmeric powder
  • 1 tsp coriander powder
  • 1/2 tsp mustard seeds
  • Curry leaves (optional)
  • 1 cup tamarind water or 1 tsp tamarind paste
  • Salt to taste
  • Fresh coriander for garnish

Steps to Make Meen Curry

  1. Heat oil in a pan and add mustard seeds.
  2. Add onions and sauté till golden.
  3. Add ginger-garlic paste and cook for 1 minute.
  4. Add tomatoes and cook till soft.
  5. Mix in chili, turmeric, and coriander powders.
  6. Pour in tamarind water and bring to a boil.
  7. Add fish pieces gently and cook for 8–10 minutes.
  8. Add salt and simmer till oil floats on top.
  9. Garnish with coriander leaves and serve hot.
